Leaky Integrate-and-Fire Neuron

A LIF neuron driven by a constant input current \(I\):

\[\tau_m \frac{dv}{dt} = -v + R \cdot I\]

When \(v \geq 1\), the neuron spikes (red lines) and resets to \(0\). The dashed grey line shows the threshold. Drag Input (x) to change the driving current. Increase it past the threshold to see regular spiking.
